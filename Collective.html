Currently under Development:SortOfAnApp

A JS intensive webpage, SortOfAnApp uses bubble-sorting techniques to collect and sort a list of items, ranging from one to two linked values. This means that it can handle single item lists, and item and quantity lists, and is expected to display the current items, and them after sorting. This should help shopping trips, spelling sesions, stat analysis, and anything else needing sorting.
